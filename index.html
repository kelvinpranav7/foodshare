About FoodShare

Hotels and event halls often have surplus edible food after functions. At the same time, NGOs and Trusts struggle to find enough food for the needy. FoodShare creates a verified communication channel with admin control to make sure food reaches the right hands on time.

How It Works

1

Donor Posts Food

Hotels/Function Halls submit leftover food details.

2

Admin Verifies

Admin checks availability and authenticity of the donor.

3

NGOs Notified

Admin calls / sends WhatsApp alerts to verified NGOs.

4

Food Collected

Authorized NGO representatives collect the food.
